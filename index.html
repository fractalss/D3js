Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The percentage of people in poverty correlates positively with smoking, obesity, and healthcare lackage.

Age has a clear positive relationship with smoking, although its relationship with obesity is less clear. Age has a negative relationship with healcare lackage.

Furthermore, household income has a negative relationship with smoking, obesity, and healthcare lackage. This is obvious, since poverty and household income are related inversely.
